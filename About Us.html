Excellence in Engineering

Founded in 2002, Pleng Limited is a leading Kenyan engineering consultancy firm. With expertise in Civil and Structural Engineering, Urban and Regional Planning, and Environmental Services, the company consistently delivers innovative and reliable solutions.
History
Pleng Limited, incorporated under the Companies Act (Cap. 486) of Kenya, is a registered and licensed engineering consultancy firm. Established in 2002, Pleng Limited has over two decades of experience delivering innovative and reliable engineering solutions. The company specializes in Civil and Structural Engineering, Urban and Regional Planning, and Environmental Services. Led by CEO James Maina Kiambigi, who has more than 20 years of experience in the field, Pleng Limited has successfully completed numerous projects in urban planning and related disciplines, significantly impacting the industry.

About Us

At Pleng Limited, we pride ourselves on delivering timely and high-quality projects across various domains. Our expertise in Civil and Structural Engineering encompasses residential houses, mansions, commercial buildings, roads, highways, and bridges. We provide comprehensive services in Urban and Regional Planning, strictly adhering to the Physical Planning Act of 1996. Our Construction Project Management services ensure commitment from preparation to completion, utilizing globally accepted techniques. Our team of qualified environmentalists conducts thorough Environmental Impact Assessments to evaluate the potential environmental effects of proposed projects. We prioritize Health, Safety, and Environment, offering integrated services in line with urban and regional planning standards. Quality and environmental audits are integral to our projects, ensuring adherence to quality control standards and minimizing environmental impacts. Additionally, we offer technical training to equip professionals with the necessary skills and knowledge.
